About

Fire-Boltt is an Indian electronics brand founded in 2015, known for affordable, feature-rich smartwatches and fitness trackers. They cater to health-conscious consumers with advanced features like heart rate and SpO2 monitoring. The brand has quickly become a leader in India's wearable tech market and has also expanded into audio products.

Fire-Boltt offers smartwatches with advanced health tracking like ECG and SpO2, along with customizable designs and long battery life.

Fire-Boltt's partnerships with celebrities and expansion into various retail channels have accelerated its rise in India's wearable tech market.
